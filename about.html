About Shangura Chemicals

Who We Are

Shangura Chemicals (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black-owned, Level 1 B-BBEE chemical manufacturing and supply company with a commitment to excellence, innovation, and transformative industrial solutions.

Founded with a vision to revolutionize chemical production in South Africa, we specialize in developing proprietary formulations that meet the unique challenges of African industries while maintaining global quality standards.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and rigorous quality control processes ensure that every product bearing the Shangura name delivers consistent performance, reliability, and value to our clients across multiple sectors.
